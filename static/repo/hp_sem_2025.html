Public Seminar (free access, no registration required)

2 October 2025

Aula, Campus Energypolis, HES-SO Valais-Wallis, Rue de l’Industrie 23, 1950 Sion

Program

16:45 — Seminar Introduction: The Role of Energy Storage in Future Power Systems (F. Sossan, HES-SO Valais-Wallis)
17:00 — Hydropower Innovation for the European Electrical System (B. Valluy, Alpiq)
17:20 — STOR-HY: Innovative storage technology and operations in hydropower (A. Presas, UPC)
17:40 — Transformation from an energy producer to a grid stabilizer (D. Fischlin, KWO)

Speakers

Fabrizio Sossan — Professor of Electrical Power Systems, HES-SO Valais–Wallis; Head of the Gridlab.
Bernard Valluy — Head of Projects and Innovation at Alpiq; 29+ years in hydropower and pumped storage projects.
